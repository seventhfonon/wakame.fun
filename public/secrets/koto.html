Level Info

Beginner: Oshi on 四, 六, 九, and 巾. Octaves on all strings.

Intermediate: Oshi on 七, 八, 十, 斗, and 為. Seconds and fifths on all strings.

Advanced: Oshi on all strings. Thirds, fourths, and sixths on all strings.
